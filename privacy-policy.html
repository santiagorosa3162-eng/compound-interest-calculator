Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This page explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used.

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Financial Calculators ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you visit our website at compound-interest-calculator-c3q.pages.dev (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes. The financial figures you enter into our calculators are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to our servers.

Last updated: March 2026

Information We Collect

We collect two categories of information:

Information you provide: The numbers and inputs you enter into our calculators (loan amounts, interest rates, retirement savings, etc.) are processed locally in your browser. This data is never transmitted to or stored on our servers. We have no access to the figures you calculate.

Analytics data collected automatically: When you visit the Site, Google Analytics (GA4) automatically collects certain technical information, including:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • General geographic location (country and region level, not precise location)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Device type (desktop, tablet, mobile)
  • Referring website or search engine
  • Interactions such as button clicks and calculator usage patterns

This information is aggregated and anonymized. We use it to understand how visitors use the Site and to improve the quality and usability of our calculators.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies — small text files placed on your device by your browser — to enable analytics and improve your experience. The cookies we use fall into the following categories:

Analytics cookies: Placed by Google Analytics to distinguish unique visitors and track usage patterns over time. These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information and are used exclusively for statistical analysis. Common Google Analytics cookies include _ga, _ga_XXXXXXXXXX, and related identifiers.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through your browser settings. You can set your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of some parts of the Site.

You can also opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Google Analytics Disclosure

This Site uses Google Analytics 4 (GA4), a web analytics service provided by Google LLC. Google Analytics uses cookies and similar technologies to collect and analyze information about the use of the Site and to report on activities and trends.

Google may also collect information about your use of other websites, apps, and online resources. You can review Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy.

We have configured Google Analytics with IP anonymization enabled, which means that Google will truncate your IP address before storing it, further protecting your privacy.
